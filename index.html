About Me

Hey! My name is Will, welcome to my portfolio page. Below you will find evidence of my budding web development career, but first, a little about me. In my day job I help school districts across Texas improve their administrative efficiences through software dedicated specifically for the K12 space. It's a noble profession and I love making an impact to help reduce the many hours school admins spend on manual workflows. You might be surprised how many school districts still use paper! Outside of work I have many hobbies. Some of them include restoring retro videogame consoles, repairing and upgrading bicycles, and running a small hot sauce business at the local farmers markets from time to time. Feel free to browse my page and reach out to me on social media any time!
