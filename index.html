About Me

Hey! My name is Will - welcome to my portfolio. Below you will find evidence of my budding web development career, but first, a little about me. In my day job, I help school districts across Texas improve administrative efficiencies through software dedicated to the K-12 space. The profession has a noble cause, and I love making an impact that helps reduce the many hours school admins spend on manual workflows. You might be surprised how many school districts still use paper to track things! Outside of work, I have many hobbies. Some of them include restoring retro videogame consoles, repairing and upgrading bicycles, and running a small hot sauce business at the local farmer's market from time to time. Feel free to check out my work below and reach out to me on social media any time!
